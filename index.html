Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Game Logic:

  1. Rock beats scissors
  2. Scissors beats paper
  3. Paper beats rock

Instructions: This game will be You v. The Computer. To play, start by selecting either rock, paper or scissors by clicking on one of the following images. Once you have made your selection, click the "Let's Play!" button. If you win the round, your win counter will increase by 1. If you lose a round, your losses counter will increase by 1. If you tie with the computer, your draws counter will increase by 1. Have fun!
